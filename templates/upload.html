How to Download Your Instagram™ Data

  1. Open Instagram™ and go to Settings.
  2. Navigate to "Accounts Center".
  3. Select "Your Information and Permissions".
  4. Click on "Download Your Information".
  5. Choose "Download or Transfer Information".
  6. Select your account and press "Next".
  7. Choose "Some of Your Information", then select "Followers and Following".
  8. Choose "Download to Device", then "Next".
  9. Set the Date Range to "All Time" and click "Create Files".
  10. Wait 3-5 minutes for the process to complete.
  11. Download .zip file
  12. Access the downloaded .zip file using WinRAR or 7-Zip.
  13. Extract the "connections" folder.
  14. Navigate to the "followers and following" folder.
  15. Upload "followers_1.html" and "following.html" files here.
